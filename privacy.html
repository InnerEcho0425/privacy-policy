Privacy Policy for InnerEcho

Effective date: April 16, 2025

InnerEcho is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. Your journal entries, moods, and other data are stored locally on your device or in your personal cloud if sync is enabled.

2. Optional Cloud Sync

If you choose to enable cloud sync, your data is encrypted and stored securely using your personal cloud provider (e.g., iCloud or Google Drive). We do not access or store this data on our servers.

3. Analytics

We may use anonymized usage analytics to help improve the app. This data does not contain personal or journal content and cannot be traced back to you.

4. Your Control

  • You control all of your data.
  • You can export, delete, or back up your data at any time from within the app.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at innerecho.journal@gmail.com.

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with a new effective date.
